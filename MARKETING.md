# BankBujhi Marketing Strategy 🇧🇩

## 🎯 Target Audience (লক্ষ্য গ্রাহক)

1.  **Young Professionals (24-35 years)**
    *   **Needs**: First credit card, travel, dining offers, salary loans.
    *   **Channels**: LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram.
2.  **Students (18-24 years)**
    *   **Needs**: Student accounts, prepaid cards (EBL Aqua), education loans.
    *   **Channels**: Facebook Groups, TikTok, YouTube.
3.  **Savers & Investors (30+ years)**
    *   **Needs**: FDR rates, DPS, secure savings.
    *   **Channels**: Facebook, Google Search, Newspapers.

---

## 📢 Marketing Channels (মার্কেটিং চ্যানেল)

### 1. Facebook (The #1 Channel in BD)
*   **Strategy**: Join popular groups like "Bank Users of Bangladesh", "Credit Card Reviews BD".
*   **Action**: Share "Card vs Card" comparison graphics.
*   **Ad Campaign**: Retargeting ads for people who visited the comparison page but didn't sign up.

### 2. SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
*   **Keywords**: "Best credit card in BD", "City Bank Amex benefits", "EBL Aqua card review".
*   **Content**: Write detailed blog posts in Bengali explaining hidden fees and benefits.

### 3. YouTube
*   **Strategy**: Create "Explainer Videos" (e.g., "কিভাবে ক্রেডিট কার্ডের বিল পরিশোধ করবেন?").
*   **Influencer Marketing**: Partner with Bangladeshi tech/financial YouTubers (e.g., PC Builders BD, Financial advisors).

---

## 🗓️ Launch Timeline (৩ সপ্তাহের পরিকল্পনা)

### Week 1: Pre-Launch (Buzz)
*   **Goal**: Collect emails.
*   **Activity**: "Waitlist" page live. Share teaser on personal Facebook/LinkedIn.
*   **Content**: "Credit Card Comparison Tool Coming Soon - Sign up for early access."

### Week 2: Soft Launch (Beta)
*   **Goal**: User feedback.
*   **Activity**: Share in closed friends group. Ask for "Bug Hunting".
*   **Incentive**: "Sign up and win a Free Financial Guide PDF".

### Week 3: Public Launch
*   **Goal**: Maximize traffic.
*   **Activity**: Post on "Bank Users of Bangladesh" group.
*   **Post format**: "I built a tool to compare all BD bank cards in one place. Let me know what you think!" (Honest, developer-focused approach works best).
