# Strategic Bangladesh Walkthrough (Part 2) 🇧🇩

This document outlines the **Business & Growth Strategy** to become the #1 financial comparison platform in Bangladesh.

## 1. 🎯 Market Positioning
**"ব্যাংক বুঝুন, সিদ্ধান্ত নিন" (Understand Banking, Take Decisions)**
- **Unique Value Prop**: "Neutral, Transparent, Bengali."
- **Competitors**: There are generic blogs, but no interactive *tools* with this level of detail.
- **Trust Factor**: "Verified by Official Sites" badge (already implemented).

## 2. 📈 Growth Channels

### A. Community Engineering
- **Facebook Groups**: "BankBujhi Community" - where users discuss hidden charges.
- **WhatsApp Channel**: Daily financial tips (very popular in BD).

### B. SEO Domination
- **Keywords**: Focus on "Low Volume, High Intent" keywords.
  - *Generic*: "Credit Card BD" (High competition)
  - *Strategic*: "EBL Aqua Card USD Limit 2025" (High conversion)

## 3. 💸 Revenue Monetization (Future)

1.  **Affiliate Leads**:
    - Partner with banks (City, BRAC, EBL) for "Verified Leads".
    - Track "Apply Now" clicks (Analytics needed).
2.  **Sponsored Cards**:
    - Allow banks to feature a "Card of the Month" (Sticky at top).
3.  **Premium Tools**:
    - "Credit Score Simulator" (Future update).

## 4. 🛡️ Trust & Compliance
- **Disclaimer**: Clearly state "Not a Bank" (Implemented in Footer).
- **Data Privacy**: No user data sold (Critical for trust).
